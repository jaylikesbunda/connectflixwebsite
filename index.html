How It Works

CollectFlix makes managing your media collection simple and effective:

1
Start by viewing your entire collection in a grid layout. Easily switch between grid and list views to customize your display.
2
Select a media cover to dive into detailed information sourced from The Movie Database, IMDb, and Rotten Tomatoes.
3
Keep your collection up-to-date automatically with data fetched from various sources, including price tracking through eBay.
4
Log price changes over time, allowing you to see historical trends for items in your collection.
5
Easily manage lending by setting media items as lent, tracking details like the lendee‚Äôs name and return date, with automatic reminders.
6
Use your smartphone, webcam, or USB barcode scanner to scan barcodes for quick entry or correction of media information.
7
Run CollectFlix as a server on your Windows PC, making it accessible to other devices on your local network for database access and media scanning.
8
Enjoy a modern, user-friendly interface with options to switch between light and dark themes for the best viewing experience.

About CollectFlix

CollectFlix is an open-source application designed to help you manage your movie and media collection with ease. Whether you're a casual collector or a dedicated enthusiast, CollectFlix provides all the tools you need to organize, track, and value your collection.
